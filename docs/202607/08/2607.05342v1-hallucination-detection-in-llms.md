## Original Abstract

We propose a novel approach for detecting hallucinations in large language models through contrastive decoding. Our method leverages the divergence between greedy and contrastive decoding outputs to identify potential factual inconsistencies. Experiments on TruthfulQA, HaluEval, and FActScore benchmarks demonstrate that our approach achieves state-of-the-art hallucination detection performance while maintaining computational efficiency.

大语言模型在生成文本时经常产生"幻觉"——即生成看似合理但实际上不正确的内容。现有的幻觉检测方法通常依赖外部知识库或需要额外的模型训练，效率低且泛化能力有限。

- **HaluEval**: 提升 4.7%
