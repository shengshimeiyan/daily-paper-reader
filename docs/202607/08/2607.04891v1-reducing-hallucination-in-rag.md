## Original Abstract

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems often suffer from hallucination when the retrieved documents conflict with the model's parametric knowledge. We propose an Adaptive Attention Mechanism (AAM) that dynamically reweights attention between retrieved contexts and model internals. Experiments on RAGAS and CRAG benchmarks show that AAM reduces hallucination rates by up to 35% while preserving answer quality.
